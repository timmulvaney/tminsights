TM Insights is an independent consultancy focused exclusively on the data centre and GPU infrastructure sector — one of the fastest-moving markets in the global economy.

We combine technical data science expertise, construction consultancy experience, deep commercial knowledge, and financial rigour to support clients across the full project lifecycle — from equipment health checks to funding strategy and physical infrastructure delivery.

Based in the UK and operating internationally, we offer the focus and responsiveness that larger organisations cannot match.

Technical depth, not just strategy

Global projects, UK rigour
